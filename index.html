DFA Yale is an interdisciplinary group of undergraduate + graduate students who design and implement local, sustainable projects with the potential for national impact. We believe in the power of Human-Centered Design to make positive social change, and regularly collaborate with the New Haven and Yale community to learn + teach design thinking strategies. We are part of a national network of passionate young thinkers moving beyond the classroom to reshape the way college students engage with our communities.
